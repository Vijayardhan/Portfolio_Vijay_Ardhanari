EchoGlove: Assistive Smart Glove for Gesture-Based Communication and Health Monitoring
DOI: 10.22214/ijraset.2025.66821
This smart glove integrates an ESP32 microcontroller with Bluetooth, a speaker for voice output, a pulse sensor for health monitoring, an ultrasonic sensor for obstacle detection, and flex sensors for hand gesture recognition. It converts gestures into speech, aids navigation, and monitors heart rate, enhancing communication and safety for individuals with speech impairments.
